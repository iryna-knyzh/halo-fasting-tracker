Halo is a calm, simple intermittent fasting tracker that helps you start a fast, reach your goal, and build a streak — without accounts, ads, or clutter.

Tap once to begin a fast and watch your progress fill toward your goal. When you finish, Halo saves the session and shows your history and stats so you can see how you're doing over time.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
• Start and end a fast with a single tap
• Choose your goal: 13, 16, 18 or 20 hours
• Watch a live timer and progress toward your goal
• See your history of past fasts
• Track your stats: total fasts, average length, goals met, longest fast
• Swipe a record to delete it

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
Halo keeps everything on your device. There are no accounts, no sign-up, and no data is collected or shared. Your fasting history never leaves your phone.

SIMPLE AND CALM
No ads. No notifications begging for attention. No subscriptions. Just a gentle, focused tool to support your fasting routine.

Halo is not a medical app and does not provide medical advice. Talk to a healthcare professional before starting any fasting routine.
