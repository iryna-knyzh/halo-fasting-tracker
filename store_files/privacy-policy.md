# Privacy Policy — Halo

_Last updated: 2026-06-22_

Halo ("the app") is a fasting tracker that respects your privacy.

## Data we collect

**None.** Halo does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

## Where your data is stored

All information you enter — your name, your fasting goal, and your fasting
history — is stored **only on your device**, using the device's local storage.
This data never leaves your phone and is not sent to us or any third party.

## Accounts

Halo has no accounts and no sign-up. You are never asked to provide an email
address, phone number, or password.

## Third-party services

Halo does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Deleting your data

You can remove your data at any time from within the app
(Settings → Clear history / Reset all data), or by uninstalling the app, which
permanently removes all locally stored information.

## Children's privacy

Halo does not collect data from anyone, including children.

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this address.
